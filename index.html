Welcome to AquaHomes

AquaHomes is a startup company offering sustainable and affordable homes, at a fair price, in a living space that floats.

AquaHomes mission is to create an eco-conscious community while protecting the waterways of Ireland. AquaHomes target market is the thousands of single people living in Ireland today who cannot afford to buy a house. An AquaHome, which is 9.2m long and 3.2, wide features a kitchen, bathroom, bedroom and a living area.

James Kennedy works primarily on this project himself, with the help of his sisters and brother who works in finance. He calls it "a Kennedy family operation."
