About AquaHomes

AquaHomes, a startup company offering sustainable and affordable homes, at a fair price, in a living space that floats.

AquaHomes founder James Kennedy took part in LaunchBox 2019, a three month start-up accelerator in Trinity College Dublin. In which they benefited from benefited from office space, mentorship, and access to Trinityâ€™s alumni, partner and investor networks.

James Kennedy works primarily on this project himself, with the help of his sisters and brother who works in finance. He calls it "a Kennedy family operation."

The idea for AquaHomes first originated when entrepreneur James Kennedy was walking by the 3 arena and saw the harbour close by. Thinking about the amount of concert goers who frequent the area, he thought if there were liveable boats on the dock, it could be rented out to people on Airbnb. However, after hearing so much about the homeless and housing crisis in Ireland and even experiencing difficulty himself finding accommodation, and decided to run the idea but to focus on providing affordable housing to the masses instead.
